⚠️ Critical Operational Intel

Zone Restriction

Farming Barbarians, Forts, or Gathering in your Home Kingdom provides 0 Honor Points. All operations must be conducted within the Lost Kingdom map.

Honor Roll Difficulty

Competing for Top Rank requires extreme AP expenditure and 24/7 activity. Assess your resources before attempting a leaderboard push.

Twilight Skin Protocol

Do not burn your entire AP reserve just for the skin if you aren't whales. The Twilight Skin can be purchased later in the Season of Conquest (SoC) shop using Lost Canyon coins.

Combat Recommendation:

Prioritize troop health for war over honor farming during pass openings.
