02 TRIPLE-MARKER PUSH: THE 3 PHASES

Phase 1: Zero-Gap Anchor

Leadership sets Marker 1. Marches assemble from scattered positions to land on the exact same pixel. This creates the "Ball" foundation.

Phase 2: Lead Advance

Marker 2 placed. T5 Tanks move into "target range" first. DPS follows but stays buried behind the blue shields to absorb accidental AOE.

Phase 3: The Engulf

The ball will move in a synchronised way and hits enemy ball—it moves through them one by one and obliterate them. This splits the enemy's marches, breaking their morale while keeping our focus at 100%.

1. Micro-Stop Technique

Essential for travel. Instead of one long click, move a short distance, STOP, and wait 1 second for the ball to compress. This prevents the "tail" from being picked off.

2. Drag-Back Maneuver

If your individual health bar drops: Do not run away. Run THROUGH your ball toward the back. Teammates will naturally "absorb" the clicks from your pursuer.

3. Zero-Gap Discipline

Every 5 seconds, even in combat, slightly adjust to stack on the leader. A ball that breathes (expands and contracts) is much harder to click than a static group.
