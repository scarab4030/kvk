03 Bag Boosts

Manual items used for specific timeframes. Choose based on your objective.

Enhanced Attack 5% / 10%

Increases Skill Damage output. Preferred for offensive murder balls.

Enhanced Defense 5% / 10%

Best for tanking marches or holding ground when being focused.

⚠️ Conflict Rule

Attack and Defense boosts DO NOT stack. Activating one cancels the other.
