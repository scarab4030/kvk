Healing Protocol

Hospital management is the difference between winning a war and going broke.

01. Maximize Buffs First

Critical Step

Before clicking the heal button, ensure your efficiency is at its maximum to save hours of speedups.

Healing Rune +15% Speed

Collect a rune from a Sanctum or Altar before starting any major healing batch.

Kingdom Buff +30% Speed

Check if the Kingdom Healing Buff is active or scheduled soon. Time your big heals with this.

02. Micro-Healing Technique

Pro Efficiency

Alliance "Helps" are your most valuable resource. Instead of healing everything at once, use micro-batches.

The Golden Timeframe

Set your heal batches for 1 hour 10 mins to 1 hour 30 mins. This range is the sweet spot where Alliance Helps (depending on your Center level) can instantly finish the timer.

Daily Help Limit

You can only receive up to 1,000 helps per day for healing. Once you hit this limit, micro-healing won't work until reset. At that point, use healing speedups to clear the hospital and return to the fight.

03. Active Combat Watch

Combat Safety

  • Monitor Capacity: If you are actively fighting, check your hospital every few minutes. If it fills up, your troops will die permanently.

  • Regular Clearing: Don't wait for a 100% full hospital. Regularly retreat to your city to clear out wounded so you always have a safety net.
