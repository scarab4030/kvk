Target Pairings

KVK 1 // Open Field Meta

Infantry

Richard I + Charles Martel

Role: The Infinite Tank

"The ultimate ball-anchor. High sustainability, but lacks damage. Best for drawing aggro in the center."

Sun Tzu + YSG

Role: AOE Nuke

"Extreme circular AOE. Must stay hidden in the ball. If targeted, retreat immediately."

Cavalry

Minamoto + Cao Cao

Role: Single Target Burst

"Glass cannon. Excellent for sniping stray marches and retreating. High speed for 1v3 flanking."

Saladin + Aethelflaed

Role: Debuff / Control

"Reduces enemy counterattack and movement. Vital for slowing down retreating enemies for the ball."

Archers

Edward + YSG

Role: High Precision Nuke

"Massive active skill damage. Extremely fragile. Requires 100% protection inside the ball."

Hermann + El Cid

Role: The Silencer

"Keeps the enemy from firing active skills. Use against high-damage commanders like Mina or Edward."
